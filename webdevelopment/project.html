Project Overview

Website Type & Purpose

This site is my personal resume, it will be my representation of who I am as an IT Professional. This site will be an alternative to sending a plain resume, as I wanted something that shows both what I know and how I best design something to communicate it. My goal is to keep this site's design clean and easy to navigate while still giving employers a full representation of my background in IT.

Home Page Overview

The home page will introduce who I am and what the site is for without overwhelming the visitor. I included a quote from Martin Luther King Jr. as the idea of doing something meaningful for others lines up with the reason why I like working in IT: you solve problems that help people in meaningful ways.

"Life's most persistent and urgent question is, 'What are you doing for others?'" – Martin Luther King Jr.

About Page Overview

The about page covers my background and where I am in my IT career. Starting at Linn State, earning my CCNA and FOA, and building hands-on experience with networking, servers, and infrastructure. It also includes my resume video for employers who want a quick overview of my background and experience.

Career Overview

The career page walks through the jobs that shaped my technical experience the most. Each position taught me something different. Whether that was managing a help desk at my local school district, working in Government with enterprise infrastructure, or learning customer facing communication in a smaller business setting. Together showing my progression on where I am going in systems administration.
