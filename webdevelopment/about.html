About Me and Education Overview

My name is Landen Lipskoch, and I am an Information Technology student with hands-on experience in networking, servers, and systems administration. I earned an Associate of Applied Science in Information Technology from State Technical College of Missouri in 2023 and hold CCNA and FOA CFOT certifications.

My background includes network installation, CAT and fiber cabling, IoT support, and maintaining physical, virtual, and cloud servers. I am especially interested in systems administration, infrastructure, and technical support.

Technical Strengths

  • Networking configuration and troubleshooting (Layer 1–3)
  • Windows Server administration
  • Hardware and software support
  • Cloud platforms including AWS and Azure
